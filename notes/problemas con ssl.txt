Problemas con los certificados ( Keychain Access )
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Descargar el certificado -> caadmin.netskope.com como .pem 
Y copiarlo en algun lugar, yo elegi ~/certs/

Configuracion permanente 

export SSL_CERT_FILE=$HOME/certs/netskope-ca.pem
export REQUESTS_CA_BUNDLE=$HOME/certs/netskope-ca.pem

Guardarlo en zsh 

echo 'export SSL_CERT_FILE=$HOME/certs/netskope-ca.pem' >> ~/.zshrc
echo 'export REQUESTS_CA_BUNDLE=$HOME/certs/netskope-ca.pem' >> ~/.zshrc
source ~/.zshrc

Test

python -c "import requests; print(requests.get('https://news.ycombinator.com').status_code)"
